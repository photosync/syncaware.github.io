Features


E2E encryption

Your data gets encrypted before leaving your device. Battle tested implementation of AES-256 GCM used by: AWS.

Media sharing

Share your encrypted albums directly from your S3 bucket. Data gets decrypted on the fly in your browser.

S3 compatible

Compatible with any S3 provider including AWS S3, Wasabi, Scaleway Object Storage, Storj, iDrive E2 or self-hosted e.g. MinIO or S3Proxy

Cost-effective

No need to run a VPS, as with Nextcloud.
Free tiers available (up to 150GB), then above Pay as you Go from $0.005/gb/mo (Backblaze).

No vendor lock-in

You own the data. S3 is an open protocol, you can always switch providers or access clients.

Privacy oriented

No trackers, no middle-man, no adverts, no external endpoints.

Browser web-client

Encryption ready, no server required, pure JavaScript. Your data doesn't leave your browser. Self-hosted version available: Github

Linux client

Mounts your S3 media files as a filesystem. Supports client-side AES-256 encryption. Currently read-only, so you can preview/backup your media locally.

Open encryption standard

Security isn't easy to get right. That's why we've used open file encryption standard proposed by AWS.

Roadmap

iOS app, Mac/Windows clients, two-way sync, support for all file types. Go to our roadmap and become part of our journey.
